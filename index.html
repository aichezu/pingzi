About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you must fend off relentless waves of monsters. Build, upgrade, and strategize to stay alive as long as possible. Whether you're on PC or mobile, enjoy smooth gameplay and intuitive controls.

  • Genre: Action, Survival
  • Platform: Web (PC & Mobile supported)
  • Controls: Keyboard, Mouse, Touch
  • Developer: CrazyGames

How to Play

Use your keyboard or touch controls to move your character, collect resources, and defeat monsters. Upgrade your weapons and defenses to survive longer and achieve a high score!

    Tips & Strategies

    As a seasoned player, you'll want to keep moving to avoid being surrounded. Focus on upgrading your weapons early and collect resources as soon as they appear. Don't forget to use obstacles to your advantage and always keep an eye on your health bar. Surviving longer means more rewards!

    Behind the Game

    Monster Survivors is a special project created by a 6-year-old boy from China and his dad. They designed this game to share the fun of survival adventures with children all over the world. The goal is to connect kids from China and other countries, letting everyone share their favorite hobbies and ideas.
